Product Name

CarCare Diary

Product Type

Mobile application for tracking vehicle maintenance history and upcoming service tasks.

The application allows car owners to log maintenance events, monitor upcoming service requirements, and maintain a structured maintenance history for their vehicle.

The system is designed to be simple for everyday drivers while also scalable for professional vehicle sellers or fleets.

Product Vision

CarCare Diary aims to become a digital maintenance diary for vehicles.

Instead of keeping service history across receipts, memory, or mechanic records, the app provides a single structured timeline of all vehicle maintenance events.

The long-term goal is to make vehicle maintenance:

easier to track

easier to maintain

easier to prove when selling a vehicle

The app also creates a verifiable maintenance record that can be shared with others.

Core Problem

Vehicle owners often struggle to track:

when services were performed

when maintenance items should be replaced

how much they spend maintaining a vehicle

what parts or fluids were used previously

Maintenance history is usually fragmented across:

paper receipts

mechanic invoices

notes apps

memory

This leads to missed service intervals, reduced vehicle lifespan, and difficulty proving maintenance history when selling a vehicle.

Core Solution

CarCare Diary provides a structured maintenance tracking system where users can:

add their vehicle

log maintenance activities

track when components need replacement

view the full history of services performed

receive reminders for upcoming maintenance

share a public link showing the vehicle’s maintenance history

Target Users
Primary Users

Individual car owners who want to maintain a clear record of their vehicle maintenance.

Typical use cases:

remembering when oil was last changed

knowing when components must be replaced

keeping a reliable maintenance history

Secondary Users
Families with Multiple Cars

Households with multiple vehicles that want to manage maintenance for each one.

Vehicle Sellers / Resellers

Individuals or businesses selling vehicles who want to demonstrate a transparent maintenance history.

This can improve buyer trust and increase resale value.

Core Value Proposition

CarCare Diary helps users:

track vehicle maintenance easily

avoid missing service intervals

keep a structured maintenance history

demonstrate proper maintenance when selling a car

MVP Scope

The current MVP focuses on the core maintenance logging workflow.

Main Capabilities

Users can:

Add a vehicle

Log maintenance events

View upcoming service needs

View maintenance history

Update vehicle mileage

Receive reminders

Share a public maintenance history link

The MVP intentionally keeps the system simple and focused.

Core Features
Vehicle Profile

Users create a vehicle profile containing:

vehicle photo

vehicle name (make and model)

year

fuel type

transmission type

current odometer value

unit preference (km or miles)

Maintenance Categories

Maintenance items are grouped into categories such as:

engine

transmission

fluids

brakes

wheels

electrical

HVAC

other

Each category contains specific maintenance items.

Example (Engine category):

engine oil

oil filter

spark plugs

air filter

timing belt

Maintenance Logs

Users can record maintenance events.

Each log contains:

log type (example: engine oil change)

odometer value at service

date

specification field (example: oil type)

notes

The system stores a history of these logs.

Maintenance Due Calculation

Each maintenance item has a predefined interval.

The app calculates when the item must be replaced again based on:

previous log

base service interval

current vehicle mileage

fuel type adjustments

The home screen shows:

"Change in X km"

When the service is approaching, a warning icon appears.

Items that are due soon appear higher in the list.

Mileage Tracking

Users can update their vehicle’s current mileage.

Mileage affects service calculations.

Maintenance History

Each maintenance item has a detailed screen showing:

current service status

warning indicator if due soon

full history of past logs

Maintenance Reminders

The app includes two basic reminder types.

Mileage Reminder

If the user has not logged mileage for a long period, the app reminds them to update it.

Service Reminder

If a maintenance item becomes due soon or overdue, the app notifies the user.

Public Maintenance History Sharing

Users can generate a public share link for their vehicle.

The share link allows others to view:

vehicle information

maintenance categories

maintenance history for each category

This is useful when selling a car to demonstrate maintenance history.
